Texas's Premier Indian
Wedding DJ

Lakha Singh, a.k.a. "DJ Lakha," is an internationally sought-after talent bringing unmatched energy to every celebration.

From luxury venues in the Texas Hill Country to the beaches of Mexico and major hubs like New York, DJ Lakha creates legendary celebrations — a master curator for all types of South Asian events, with a special passion for the rich tapestry of Indian weddings, from intimate Sangeets to grand Receptions.

His ability to read any crowd, combined with his skills as a trained percussionist, allows him to create a vibrant atmosphere that captivates every generation. Unlike larger DJ companies, you get DJ Lakha himself — a boutique, personalized experience every time.
