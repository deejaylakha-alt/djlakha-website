# Google Business Profile (GBP) Setup Guide for DJ LAKHA

## Why This Is Critical
Google Business Profile is the **#1 factor** for ranking in Google Maps' "Local 3-Pack." When someone searches "Indian Wedding DJ near me" in Texas, GBP determines if you appear in results.

---

## Step 1: Claim Your Profile

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
2. Sign in with a Google account (recommend: using `info@djlakha.com` if it's Gmail, or create a dedicated one)
3. Search for "DJ LAKHA" — if it exists, click **Claim this business**
4. If not, click **Add your business to Google**
5. Complete verification (postcard, phone, or email)

## Step 2: Optimize Your Profile

### Business Name
```
DJ LAKHA | Indian Wedding DJ & Entertainment
```

### Business Category
- **Primary:** DJ Service
- **Additional:** Wedding DJ, Entertainment Agency, Event Planner

## Step 4: Reviews Strategy

### Getting Your First 10 Reviews
1. Start with past clients from HoneyBook (use `auto-emailer.js`)
2. Ask family/friends who've attended events
3. Ask wedding planners you've worked with

